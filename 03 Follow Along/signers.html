Who were the signers?

famous signers

  • Thomas Jefferson - Primary author of the Declaration of Independence. Third President of the United States.
  • John Adams - Strong advocate for independence, second President of the United States, key figure in the Continental Congress.
  • Benjamin Franklin - Diplomat, inventor, and one of the most influential Founding Fathers. The oldest signer at 70 years old.
  • John Hancock - President of the Continental Congress, famous for his bold and large signature.
  • Samuel Adams - Leader of Massachusetts patriots, outspoken critic of British rule, and organizer of the Boston Tea Party.
  • Richard Henry Lee - Proposed the resolution for independence and formally introduced the resolution in the Continental Congress.
  • Thomas Paine - Though not a signer, Paine's pamphlet *Common Sense* was influential in garnering support for independence.
  • Francis Lewis - New York signer, lost his wealth and home during the Revolution. His wife was captured by the British.
  • William Floyd - New York signer, helped secure resources for the American Revolution.
  • Matthew Thornton - Signer from New Hampshire, was the last to affix his name to the document.
